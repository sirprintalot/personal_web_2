Hello

I'm a drummer born in Cochabamba, Bolivia in 1990. He began his musical studies at the age of ten at the Eduardo Laredo Institute where he began studying violin and later specialized in percussion and drums. As of 2012, he resides in the city of Buenos Aires where he began his studies in the upper cycle of the Popular Music School of Avellaneda. In 2014 he entered the jazz career at the Manuel de Falla Conservatory and concluded his studies in 2017. He studied drums, ensemble and other subjects with teachers: Diego Lutteral, Ezequiel "Chino" Piazza, Eloy Michelini, Sergio Verdinelli, Ernesto Jodos, Hernán Merlo, Juan Pablo Arredondo, Valentín Reiners, Daniel Johansen, Enrique Norris, among others. He participated in jazz residencies by: Devin Hoff (USA), Tim Berne (USA), Michael Formanek (USA). He was also part of an ensemble led by Gregory Burk (USA) within the framework of the Jazz Festival of the city of Buenos Aires 2016. In 2017 he was part of an international crossing at the Buenos Aires Jazz Festival together with Rodrigo Agudelo (Arg.), Evgeny Ring (Rus.) and Robert Lucaciu (German). He was part of the Big Band of the Manuel de Falla Conservatory, sharing the stage with musicians such as: Michael Formanek (USA) and Ralph Alessi (USA). He recorded and shared the stage with musicians such as: Grilo Villegas, Quimbando, Tupay, Mala Traza, Mao Khan, Chelo Arias, Martin Iaies quartet, Lucas Rochaix quintet, among others. He is currently active in various groups related to jazz, original music, improvisation and also works as a teacher and session musician in both Argentina and Bolivia.
